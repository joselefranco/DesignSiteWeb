Rock The Blues

A website on blues and blues-rock.

Why Did We Build This Website?

Throughout time, the world has witnessed several musical revolutions. Some gave grounds for new musical genres while others deeply affected popular culture. Blues & Blues Rock are perfect examples of the latter.

These musical genres have changed the way we consume music and have democratized the use of certain instruments as the electric guitar.

Creating an informational website concerning these specific types of music not only seems logical but also important. Let's bring back the good old days when music was not about auto-tuning and using MIDI keyboard controllers.

It's time to make the '20s and the '60s great again!
